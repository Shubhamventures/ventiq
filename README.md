# VENTIQ

## AI-Powered Operating System for Private Capital

VENTIQ is building the intelligence layer for private capital.

Our mission is to help fund managers, finance teams, investors, and administrators manage funds from a single AI-powered platform.

### Core Areas

* Fund Management
* Investor Management
* Portfolio Management
* Compliance Management
* Accounting & Operations
* Capital Calls & Distributions
* Document Intelligence
* AI Copilot

### Vision

To become the operating system for venture capital, private equity, debt funds, family offices, and alternative investment funds.
